translation_coherence vocabolary

equivalent: predicate indicating that the subject and the object are referring to the same entity and they are equal
synonymy: predicate indicating that the subject and the object are referring to the same entity and they are synonyms

Expression: class for objects connected to portions of text that allow to make statements about them
same_expression: predicate that indicate that the subject and the object are two expressions referring to the same portion of text
involves_verb: predicate that connects an expression to the verb
involves_aux: predicate that connects an expression to the auxiliary verb
